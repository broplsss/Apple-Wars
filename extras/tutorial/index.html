Basics

With Mouse

Look around with your mouse.

Hold down to move towards your pointer.

Shoot with the F key, and leave a game with A.

On Touchscreen

Hold down to change direction and move towards your pointer.

Shoot with the button, and leave a game with .

Gameplay

Poison

Some bullets can poison the enemy, such as cherry pits.

When poisoned, you take a decreasing amount of damage until the effect wears off.

Explosives

Some bullets explode on impact, including the pear missile.

When something explodes, it deals extra damage to surrounding apples, and to the main target.

Splitting

A few bullets split into more bullets on impact, like the raspberry.

The fragments go off in all directions, and always do less damage than whatever they came from.

When a watermelon splits, the watermelon slices that come from it also split on impact.

Winning

One full game takes about one and a half minutes, with the enemies spawning faster and faster as you get closer to the end.

The last 20 seconds stop increasing in difficulty. Bosses don't spawn, but there are over five new enemies every second.

Shopping

Bullets

If the box under the icon is gray and has a price on it, you haven't bought it yet.

If the item has the unknown icon (gray apple with a ?), then you have to buy something else to reveal this bullet.

Once bought, the bullet will either have a button saying EQUIP or EQUIPPED. Equipped means this is the bullet you're currently using.

Upgrades

The green bar behind the price - if any - represents how many levels of the upgrade you bought.

The upgrades do all sorts of things, from increasing damage to giving you poison resistance.

Options

Settings

All of the settings are pretty self explanitory. Change them by clicking the switch button.
